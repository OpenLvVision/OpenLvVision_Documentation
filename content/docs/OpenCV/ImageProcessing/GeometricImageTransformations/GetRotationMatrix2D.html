OpenLvVision_OpenCv.lvlib:GetRotationMatrix2D.vi

Calculates an affine matrix of 2D rotation.

The function calculates the following transformation matrix:

{{< katex >}}$ \begin{bmatrix} \alpha & \beta & (1-\alpha)\cdot \texttt{center.x} - \beta \cdot \texttt{center.y} \\ -\beta & \alpha & \beta \cdot \texttt{center.x} + (1-\alpha)\cdot \texttt{center.y} \end{bmatrix} ${{< /katex >}}

where

{{< katex >}}$ \begin{aligned} \alpha &= \texttt{scale} \cdot \cos(\texttt{angle}) \\ \beta &= \texttt{scale} \cdot \sin(\texttt{angle}) \end{aligned} ${{< /katex >}}

The transformation maps the rotation center to itself. If this is not the desired behavior, adjust the translation (shift) terms accordingly.

For detailed information, please refer to the OpenCV documentation
