OpenLvVision_OpenCv.lvlib:Multiply.vim

Calculates the per-element scaled product of two arrays.

The function multiply calculates the per-element product of two arrays:

  • Element-wise multiplication with optional scaling:
    {{< katex >}}$ \texttt{dst}(I) = \texttt{saturate}(\texttt{scale} \cdot \texttt{src1}(I) \cdot \texttt{src2}(I)) ${{< /katex >}}

Where I is the multi-dimensional index of array elements. Each channel is processed independently in multi-channel arrays.

{{< alert context="warning" text="Saturation is not applied when the arrays have the depth CV_32S. You may even get a negative value in the case of overflow." />}}

For detailed information, please refer to the OpenCV documentation
