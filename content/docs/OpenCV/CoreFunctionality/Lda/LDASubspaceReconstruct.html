OpenLvVision_OpenCv.lvlib:LDASubspaceReconstruct.vim

Reconstructs the original high-dimensional data from the low-dimensional projected space. This is the inverse operation of SubspaceProject. It is useful for visualizing what the compressed data represents or for noise reduction.

For detailed information, please refer to the OpenCV documentation
