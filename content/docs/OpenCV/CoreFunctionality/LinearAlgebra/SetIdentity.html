Initializes a scaled identity matrix.

The function cv::setIdentity initializes a scaled identity matrix:

{{< katex >}}$ \texttt{mtx}(i,j)= \begin{cases} \texttt{value}, & \text{if } i=j \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} ${{< /katex >}}
